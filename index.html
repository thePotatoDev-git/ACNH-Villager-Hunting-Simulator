Animal Crossing Villager Hunting Simulator

Have you ever been without your Nintendo Switch and wanted to experience looking for a villager you wanted? Try to find your favorite villager while having no control of what villager you'll actually run into. How many times will you run into Barold? How about Rodney? Pretend to waste all your Nook Miles Tickets and run into hundreds to thousands of villagers before you finally find the elusive Raymond you've always wanted!
